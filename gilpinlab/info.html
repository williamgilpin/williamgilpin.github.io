Visit the PMA Building at UT Austin

The Physics, Math, and Astronomy (PMA) building is located at:

PMA Building
2515 Speedway
Austin, TX 78712
United States

For visitors, the PMA building is easily accessible from the main entrance on Dean Keeton. Parking is available at nearby garages, and public transportation options include several bus routes that stop close to the building. The ground floor is floor 4 on the elevators. Once inside, we are located on the 14th floor.

Loading Dock Information

The loading dock at the PMA building is open from 9:00 AM to 4:30 PM on weekdays, except for holidays. The dock is elevated, and there is a freight elevator available for transporting large items to different floors.
